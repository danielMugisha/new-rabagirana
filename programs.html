Souls could shine brighter than the sun

We conduct a 3-day interactive workshop based on the Bible to enable people to experience healing of their wounds and reconcile at the cross. We train facilitators to enable them to run workshops. Schools are a combination of workshops, training, and practicums. We encourage attendants to repackage the topics in artistic formats like songs or paintings or integrate HHTN into community development processes. For more info. see the article "Introducing the Reconciliation Ministry" and the video.

We conduct 3-days of basic training on Biblical principles of development and an additional 3days of practical outworking that include methods of tackling poverty, domestic violence, caring for the environment, and other topics that will result in initiatives leading to holistic transformation. Together with Resonate Global Mission, we have also introduced "Helping without Hurting," a 3-day training manual for churches and organizations that run community development programs.

We conduct a 3-day training on worldview and an introduction to Biblical leadership, character study, leadership skills, team dynamics, and conflict transformation. Recently, in partnership with Resonate Global Mission, we introduced the Timothy Leadership Training Initiative, a 2-year program with 6 modules for church leaders with or without theological training. Trainees attend for four days in three months and carry out assignments.
