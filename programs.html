Unity Mountain is an initiative launched by Rabagirana Ministries in the three communities of Rusheshe, Mbabe, and Ayabaraya. Its mission is to promote long-lasting peace by fostering healing, development, education, environmental care, health, sports, and culture. The project brings people together from different backgrounds to build unity and transform their communities.

Healing Christian Witness

This pillar focuses on emotional and spiritual healing among genocide survivors and those who were involved in the atrocities. It includes trauma healing workshops, faith-based support networks, and engagement with church members to encourage everyday acts of peace. Youth and people from diverse historical backgrounds are brought together to listen, heal, and walk toward reconciliation./p>

Development

Communities are trained in leadership and community-based development. Small business groups are formed to promote self-reliance and economic growth. These groups include liquid soap production, craft making, mushroom farming, vase pottery, and tailoring. The goal is to build strong, self-sustaining communities through cooperation and creativity.

Education

Unity Mountain provides literacy programs for adults, computer literacy training, and values-based education for school-going children through the Munyamahoro Program. The aim is to open doors of opportunity and equip all generations with knowledge and life skills that foster peace and Unity.

Environment

Environmental activities include distributing fruit tree seedlings, Building kitchen gardens, and soil erosion control. Community members are also engaged in awareness campaigns to encourage environmental protection. These efforts aim to improve both the health of the land and the wellbeing of its people.

Health, Sport and Culture

This area includes a community cultural hub, a women's football team made up of young mothers, and a traditional dance troupe named Inganzo y’Ubumwe (The Inspiration of Unity). These initiatives provide physical, emotional, and cultural outlets for expression, joy, and healing—bringing people together across generations and backgrounds.

Rwanda Unite is a reconciliation pillar by Rabagirana Ministries that brings together survivors, perpetrators, communities through healing workshops, prison outreach, national forums, and media campaigns. Interventions through partner organizations and churches across Rwanda foster forgiveness, resilience, and unity and our slogan is “one heart one community at a time” we believe that if Rwanda can be healed then it is possible in other nations too!

Prison Ministry

Despite COVID-19 challenges, “Sowers of Love” groups continued offering pastoral and psychosocial support to inmates, showing remarkable resilience. Detained women engaged in income-generating crafts, with some supporting their families and children's education. As activities resumed, Rabagirana was granted space to train new facilitators and prepare long-term inmates for reintegration, including genocide-related cases.

Universities

Rabagirana partnered with CEP and GBU to train 125 facilitators who reached over 26,000 students with healing and reconciliation efforts. Campus activities included workshops, unity clubs, and dialogue sessions. Many participants later became church leaders, reflecting the program’s lasting spiritual influence.

Churches and Organizations

In partnership with Kicukiro churches, Rabagirana brought together genocide survivors and perpetrators to form solidarity groups promoting reconciliation. Some groups evolved into cooperatives and youth clubs, fostering lasting unity. A Gahanga group built Iriba ry’imbabazi (“Spring of Mercy”), a shared water source now benefiting over 200 community members.

Trainings

In Training ,Rabagirana is launching CECECE to offer leadership, counseling, community development, and discipleship curricula across Rwanda. The center will be promoted nationwide, inviting financial support for wider access. A resource unit and multimedia studio will help extend its reach beyond Rwanda through digital platforms

Walking alongside you to wholeness - At Emmaus Family Centre, we are committed to walking alongside individuals, families, and organizations on their journey toward healing, resilience, and transformation. Through our range of services, we foster emotional well-being and empower people to thrive in every sphere of life.

DEBRIEFING WORKSHOPS

Our debriefing sessions are a vital initiative to support the well-being of individuals across various organizations. By creating a safe and structured environment for participants to process their experiences, we help foster healthier, more resilient teams capable of navigating challenges and contributing meaningfully to their organizations.

Psychoeducation

Workshops and training sessions designed to educate and equip families with essential skills. Topics include parental classes, trauma counseling, emotional well-being, stress management, and communication skills to strengthen family bonds.

Pre-Marital Counseling & Marriage Enrichment

Helping couples build a strong foundation for a lasting and fulfilling marriage through guided sessions. We also offer marriage enrichment activities for couples seeking to enhance their relationship, resolve conflicts, and improve communication.
