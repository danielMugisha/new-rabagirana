About Rabagirana Ministries

Rabagirana Ministries is dedicated to seeing Rwanda become a model of lasting peace, unity, and light for the nations to the glory of God. Our mission is to equip Christian workers to become catalysts of healing, reconciliation, and community transformation.

Through various programs, we aim at achieving our objectives in conflicts resolution, healing, reconciliation, and Christian personal development.
